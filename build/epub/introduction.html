Introduction

This note is based on a course for newcomers in the spring of 2014 at the Tokyo institute of Technology. See also the free math textbook [https://github.com/phasetr/math-textbook].

We talk about linear algebra, especially it’s relation to calculus, i.e., differentiation and integration.

In this talk we speak in Japanese but write in English on blackboard. It is because I want newcomers to get used to writing and reading in English.
